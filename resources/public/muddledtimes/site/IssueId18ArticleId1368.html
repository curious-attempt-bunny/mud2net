Section:Interviews

Ring of stones.
You find yourself overawed by the huge ring of stones which encircle this, the highest
part of the island rock. As the wind sweeps over from the rugged cliffs to the east and
north, the desolation of this place is all-pervading. To the south and west are steep
slopes downwards. Far, far away in the distance on the southeast horizon can be glimpsed
the sail of a wrecked ship. In the centre of the ring of stones is a large, heavy,
slate, sacrificial slab, still stained by the blood of the multitudes sacrificed upon it
in druidic times. The slate slab looks too heavy for you to move. 
Noodles the warlock is here.

(Karya the melancholy witch) asks "When did you start playing MUD2?".
Noodles the warlock says "about 6 months before WP shut down".
Noodles the warlock says "not sure how long back that is".
(Karya the melancholy witch) says "That'll probably make it Autumn 2000".

Noodles the warlock says "I found out about MUD from my brother".

Noodles the warlock says "Mike".

Noodles the warlock says "Well, it is my real name and I'm not that good at

Noodles the warlock says "well it was quite hard to learn at first but back when

influence on you?".
Clouds are gathering overhead.

Noodles the warlock says "most of the wizards and witches that were on during that

(Karya the melancholy witch) asks "Are there any PKs that you especially remember?".
Noodles the warlock says "yea, Foz, Droo, Azrael and Po I seem to remember".

Noodles the warlock says "I think it is OK but when it happens all the time it's

(Karya the melancholy witch) asks "What prompts you to PK?".

Noodles the warlock says "not sure but if all the old players came back it might

(Karya the melancholy witch) asks "Is there anything else you'd like to say?
