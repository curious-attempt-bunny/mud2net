In this issue Foddy gives us news about the special Christmas offer that gives players a chance to give free time to their friends. See News & Gossip for more details.

Please send in your articles, musings and mudmeet photos and reviews to editor@muddled-times.com for the next issue in February, and if nothing else, please fill in the Feedback Form!
