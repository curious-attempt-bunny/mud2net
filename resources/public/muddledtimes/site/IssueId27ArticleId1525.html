Top 100, Dead or Alive

This chart shows the top 100 mortals who have played over the last three months (1st June - 31st August 2004).

The top 100 represents the highest score that each individual player achieved at any point in time during the last three months, regardless of whether they have died later. You also get to see the players' level (and even prefix) at the time they reached their highest score.

Interesting facts for this issue:

  • S & M are the most popular initals for players in the top 100 - with 22% between them!
  • We have two players of the month in the top 100 this issue!
  • "Jincks the bad penny" (8th) - could this be a recently discovered level?

I hope you enjoyed this issue's charts!
